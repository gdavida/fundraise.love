Prescott Events

There's so many ways to be a part of the Prescott Community!

There's almost always something going on at Prescott for our students and families to enjoy. And there's so many ways to join in the fun! You can volunteer, or you can come participate, and often you can purchase snacks and other treats from the PTO concession stands. Check out our event calendar below.



Local business nights

Enjoy dinner out during a Prescott night!

Check out the calendar for information on dates and locations of local business' Prescott nights. Come enjoy a family night out with other Prescott families, all while helping to support your Prescott PTO.
